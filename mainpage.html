Scholarship Managment System

Christ has Three Categories

Achievement Scholarship
This Scholarship is execlusive awarded for students who are excellent in academics. Students acdemic excellence will be evaluated each semester on a continuos basis and students will be selected for this scholarship after evaluated of performance in the previous semsters. Students should have minimum of 85 attendance in each course in the pervious semester and minimum of 75% marks aggregatr.
Talent Scholarship
This scholarship is provided for encouraging students to excel in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities (cultural, sports & games and inter-collegiate fests) Students should have minimum 80% of attendance in each course in the previous semester Students should secure minimum 60% of marks (aggregate of CIA & End Semester Examinations) and should have passed in all the courses Students should have represented the college and won at least one prize at the University level/State Level /National level competitions.
Upliftment Scholarship
This scholarship is provided to financially disadvantaged students Students' family annual income should be equal to or less than Rs. one lakh Student should satisfy the Scholarship Committee's queries and convince the need for scholarship Students need to produce the proof of income certificate given by the local civil authority Students should have minimum 80% of attendance in each course in the previous semester Students should secure minimum 60% of marks ( aggregate of CIA & End Semester Examinations) and should have passed in all the courses
